Research collaboration request

Debugging scenarios in Behavioral Programming

We are collecting representative bugs in Behavioral Programming programs to improve and evaluate an LLM-agent based workflow for debugging BP models.

The four examples below are candidate bug patterns we found so far. We would appreciate additional scenarios, variants, edge cases, and examples from people familiar with BP.

Review the scenarios

The task

Help us expand the bug collection

What to send

Any BP debugging scenario in free form: code, explanation, expected behavior, wrong traces, screenshots, diagrams, images, or short notes.

Timing

We plan to keep this collection phase open for two weeks. A reminder will be sent after about a week and a half.

Goal

The goal is to build a higher-quality benchmark of BP bugs for evaluating debugging methods, explanations, and LLM-agent repair workflows.

Methodology

Our methodology studies how agentic LLM workflows inspect BP event traces, reason about synchronization behavior, explain faults, and propose repairs.

Acknowledgments

Thank you for helping shape the benchmark

We thank the BP community, researchers, lecturers, and practitioners who review these examples and contribute additional bugs. Your feedback will help make the resulting dataset more realistic and useful.
